note : "The two printed editions, Impr. 1516 and Impr. 1523 contain the same redaction (one could add to the tree a potential descent from the first to the second), and five copies from each are known. Private manuscripts and the fragments are not shown on the stemmata, as well as mutilated or lacunary, abridged manuscripts and foreign translations. There are probably more manuscripts than the one listed there. Manuscripts are splitted in three groups: 14 for the short version, 13 for the long version, and 16 mix both versions. Ponceau gives 5 stemmata, the first for short version; the second for long version, part one; the third for long version, part two;  the fourth and fifth again for long version, part one and two, but this time including two mixed manuscripts that can be placed for a part of their text. This file is for the stemma of the short version."
